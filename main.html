Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is renowned for its stunning temples, traditional wooden houses, and beautiful gardens. A city that blends history with culture, it offers a glimpse into Japan's past while being a hub for art and gastronomy.

  1. Kinkaku-ji - The stunning Golden Pavilion surrounded by beautiful gardens.
  2. Fushimi Inari Taisha - Famous for its thousands of torii gates winding up the mountain.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A mesmerizing walk through towering bamboo stalks.
  4. Gion District - Experience traditional Geisha culture in this historic area.
  5. Nijo Castle - A UNESCO World Heritage Site showcasing Japanese architecture.
  • Ramen - A must-try dish, served with rich broth and various toppings.
  • Sushi - Fresh and delicious, experience the true art of Japanese cuisine.
  • Yudofu - A simple yet flavorful tofu hot pot perfect for cold days.
  • Matcha - Try this famous powdered green tea in various sweets or drinks.
  • Mochi - Soft rice cakes filled with sweet bean paste or ice cream.
